A checkout flow rebuilt for scale

Simplified a multi-currency, multi-tier payment journey for a multinational fintech group — from research to a reusable design system component.

[ Project visual — payment flow screens ]
Context
A multi-currency, multi-tier payment journey was showing a high drop-off rate at checkout.
Constraint
Compliance requirements and legacy back-end logic that couldn't be rebuilt on this timeline.
Decision
I redesigned the flow around one reusable design system component, instead of a one-off screen fix.
Outcome
Shipped a pattern that's now reused across three product lines within the group.
