Brand identity built for 300 km/h

Brand assets for Wolf Warriors IT Technology's sponsorship, deployed on the rider suit and the official race motorcycle for a full MotoGP season.

[ Project visual — livery / rider suit ]
Context
Wolf Warriors IT Technology's sponsorship marks needed to work across livery, rider suit, and broadcast footage at the same time.
Constraint
MotoGP placement regulations, plus legibility at race speed, filmed in motion.
Decision
I built a reduced mark system — one that held its shape whether printed small or moving fast.
Outcome
Deployed on the official race motorcycle and rider suit for the season.
